In Part 1, we covered the data doom loop [https://hasura.io/blog/escaping-the-data-doom-loop-in-financial-services] – the cycle of complexity, inefficiency, and eroding trust in financial data that persists despite massive investments. The solution isn’t another sweeping architectural overhaul. It’s about fixing how data is defined, accessed, and governed at the source.
